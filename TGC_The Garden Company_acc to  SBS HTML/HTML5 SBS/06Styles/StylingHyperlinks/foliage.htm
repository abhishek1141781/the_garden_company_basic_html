Diagnosing Foliage Problems

Here's a quick guide to diagnosing common problems with plant foliage:

  1. Yellow
    • Wet soil
    • Low fertility
    • Root rot pathogens
    • Roots cut or damaged
      • Gophers
      • Moles
      • Insects
    • Nematodes
  2. Necrotic spots
    • Round spots
      • Fungal leafspot
    • Angular spots
      • Bacterial leafspot
  3. White powder
    • Powdery mildew
  4. Light and dark green areas
    • Random patterns
      • Virus
    • In rows
      • Nutrient deficiency
  5. Holes or chewed areas
    • Insect injury
